1/ Most AI assistants are useless. Not because they're dumb. Because they optimize for engagement, not outcomes.

An AI that gives 5 options isn't thinking. It's decision paralysis.

2/ Here's the problem: We measure the wrong things.

✗ Page views
✗ Time spent
✗ User engagement
✗ Response speed

None of this equals usefulness.
